INSTRUCTIONS

Try to hit the "Target Score" by clicking on the random crystals. The "Target Score" will be different every time the game resets.

Each crystal will give you a different amount of points and will also reset every time you win or lose the game.

The "Target Score" will always be between 19 and 120.

Each crystal has a random value between 1 and 12.
